Hi Pioneers!

Thank you for helping us improve the Neura iOS app experience and finding bugs.

Neura uses complex artificial intelligence to try to understand what’s going on in your life. We say “try” because right now we don’t always get it right. We need you to tell us when we get it wrong so we can improve.

For the next 2 weeks, your mission is to report when certain events occur in your life. Ready? Here’s how!

STEP 1: Download our App

STEP 2: Sign up to the app

STEP 3: Insert your home and work locations as requested

STEP 4: Respond to the push notification sent to you by the Pioneers app. Every time an Event is detected, we send you a notification with 4 optional answers:

  • Event has happened

  • Event has happened but at a different time

  • Event has happened but not the right event

  • Event has happened but not the right event

All you need to do is pick the right answer and send it to us. If you are not sure about the answer – please ignore the notification! This is important since inaccurate answers may lead to inaccurate results

STEP 5: Report your events right before they occur in the Neura app. The closer you report your event to when it actually occurs, the more accurate Neura gets. Please report when you:

  • Arrive and leave your home

  • Arrive and leave your work

  • Start and finish driving – any type of driving (train, car, bicycle, as a passenger or as a driver)

  • Start and finish running

  • Start and finish walking

  • Wake up and go to sleep

To report your event in the app:

  • Pick an event from the main screen

  • Confirm the event and time

STEP 6: Get Paid. To get full payment, you must:

  • Report events for 10 or more days

  • Send 10 or more daily summary feedbacks

  • Have our app installed on your main phone

  • Use an iPhone iOS 8 or up

  • Your iPhone has 3G or up

  • Keep on your GPS (location), Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth on during the testing period

  • Your home and work are 2 separate locations, and over 50 meters apart

  • You have cellular reception at home

  • NOTE: Lack of compliance will disqualify you from getting full payment.
