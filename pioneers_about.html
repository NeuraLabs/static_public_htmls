Hi Pioneers!

Thank you for helping us improve the Neura iOS app experience and finding bugs.

Neura uses complex artificial intelligence to try to understand whats going on in your life. We say try because right now we dont always get it right. We need you to tell us when we get it wrong so we can improve.

For the next 2 weeks, your mission is to report when certain events occur in your life. Use our app to report, and also fill out a daily summary at the end of each day. Ready? Heres how!

STEP 1: Download our App

STEP 2: Report your events right before they occur in the Neura app. The closer you report your event to when it actually occurs, the more accurate Neura gets. Please report when you:

       Arrive and leave your home

       Arrive and leave your work

       Start and finish driving any type of driving (train, car, bicycle, as a passenger or as a driver)

       Start and finish running

       Start and finish walking

       Wake up and go to sleep

To report your event in the app:

       Pick an event from the main screen

       Confirm the event and time

       If needed, you have 30 mins to change the report

       If we detect an event you have not reported, we will send you a reminder to report it. Please try to report all events, it really helps us with our accuracy.

STEP 3: Fill out a daily summary report. In the app, you will get a report at 8pm every night that lists all the events Neura guessed occurred in your day. Check it over and submit your completed feedback before 7pm the next day to get full payment. Accuracy is key! Check this report while events are still fresh in your mind:

       Confirm each correct event by tapping the V next to the event name

       Edit incorrect events by tapping edit. Write a short note explaining what actually happened. EXAMPLE: I was at the movies, not at home, I didnt fall asleep -- I was still watching tv, I was driving my car, not running, etc.

       Edit events labeled Data quality was low by telling us what you actually did EXAMPLE: I was watching tv at home, I was driving my car, etc.

       Make sure all events are now correctly described and submit your daily summary

STEP 4: Get Paid To get full payment, you must:

       Report events for 10 or more days

       Send 10 or more daily summary feedbacks

       Have our app installed on your main phone

       Use an iPhone iOS 8 or up

       Your iPhone has 3G or up

       Keep on your GPS (location), Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth on during the testing period

       Your home and work are 2 separate locations, and over 50 meters apart

       You have cellular reception at home

       NOTE: Lack of compliance will disqualify you from getting full payment.
